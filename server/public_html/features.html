How to sign up

To sign up press the sign up botton on the right side panel to slide the section over to the sign up page, then proceed to enter your credentials



How to login

To login in after you sign up press the sign in button on the left panel and wait for it to slide over and then enter your credentials



How to navigate the upper nav bar

To navigate the upper nav bar you can click on the home button to go back to the home page, the cog button to go to the settings page, and the profile button to go to your profile page



How to navigate the left nav bar

To navigate the left nav bar you can click on the new project button to create a new project



Grid and list

To switch between the grid and list view you can click on the grid or list button on the top right of the page



Project Cards

To navigate the project cards you can click on the project card to go to the project page
